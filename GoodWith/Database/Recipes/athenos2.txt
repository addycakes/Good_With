                            Applesauce Muffins

Recipe By     :n/a
Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Breads/Rolls                    Muffins


  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1 1/4           cups  flour
  1         tablespoon  Calumet Baking Powder
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
  2               cups  Post Fruit & Fibre Cereal
  1                cup  fat free milk
  1                     egg
     1/2           cup  applesauce
     1/3           cup  brown sugar - (firmly packed)
  2        tablespoons  margarine -- melted

Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Spray muffin pan with no stick cooking spray.

Mix flour, baking powder and salt in large bowl.  Mix cereal and milk in another bowl; let stand 3 minutes.

Beat egg in small bowl; stir in applesauce, sugar and margarine.  Stir into cereal mixture.  Add to flour mixture; stir just until moistened.  (Batter will be lumpy.)

Spoon batter into prepared muffin pan, filling each cup 2/3 full.  Bake 20 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve warm.

This recipe yields 12 muffins.

Storage Know-How:  Store completely cooled muffins in airtight container up to 2 days.

                       Baked Feta Cheese Appetizer

Recipe By     :n/a
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Appetizers

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1            package  refrigerated crescent dinner rolls -- (4 oz)
  1            package  Athenos Traditional Feta Cheese - (8 oz)
     1/2      teaspoon  dried oregano
  1                     egg -- beaten

Unroll dough on lightly greased cookie sheet; press seams together to form 12- by 4-inch rectangle.

Sprinkle feta cheese with oregano; lightly press oregano into feta cheese.  Place feta cheese, oregano-side up, in center of dough.  Bring edges of dough up over feta cheese; press edges together to seal, completely enclosing feta cheese.  Brush with egg.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until lightly browned.  Serve with crackers, French bread or fresh fruit slices.

This recipe yields 8 servings.

Great Substitute:  Try 1 package (8-oz) Athenos Feta Cheese with Basil & Tomato, Garlic & Herb or Peppercorn in place of Traditional Feta Cheese.

                             Black Bean Soup

Recipe By     :n/a
Serving Size  : 7     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Soups/Stews

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  2               cans  black beans - (15 oz ea) -- undrained, divided
  3               cups  water
     1/2           cup  chopped onion
  1           envelope  Good Seasons Italian or
                        Garlic & Herb Salad Dressing Mix
  1 1/2           cups  Minute Instant Brown Rice -- uncooked
                        Breakstone's or Knudsen Light Sour Cream
                        Cilantro leaves
                        Chopped red pepper

Place 1 1/2 cans of the beans with liquid in blender container; cover.  Blend until smooth.  Pour into large saucepan.  Stir in remaining beans, water, onion and salad dressing mix.

Bring to boil.  Stir in rice.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 5 minutes.

Spoon into individual serving bowls; top with remaining ingredients.

This recipe yields 7 (1-cup) servings.

                             Caesar Potatoes

Recipe By     :n/a
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Side Dish

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1           envelope  Good Seasons Gourmet Caesar or
                        Italian Salad Dressing Mix
     1/4           cup  vinegar
  3        tablespoons  oil
  1 1/2         pounds  red potatoes -- cut into chunks
  1             medium  red or green pepper -- cut into 1" pieces
  1             medium  onion -- cut into 1" pieces

Prepare salad dressing mix as directed on envelope with vinegar and oil, omitting water.  Toss remaining ingredients with dressing mixture.  Spoon evenly onto double layer of heavy-duty aluminum foil; close foil to form tightly-sealed pouch.

Place on grill over medium coals.  Grill 30 to 35 minutes or until tender, turning halfway through grilling time to prevent burning.

This recipe yields 6 (3/4-cup) servings.

To Bake: Prepare as directed except for grilling.  Spoon evenly into 15- by 10- by 1-inch baking pan lined with foil.  Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes or until tender.

                       Shrimp With Tomato And Feta

Recipe By     :n/a
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Seafood                         Shellfish


  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  chopped onion
  2        tablespoons  olive oil
  1                can  Italian-style plum tomatoes - (28 oz) -- drained, cut up
     1/3           cup  dry white wine
  2          teaspoons  dried oregano leaves
     3/4         pound  medium shrimp -- cleaned
  1            package  Athenos Traditional Crumbled Feta Cheese -- (4 oz)
  2        tablespoons  chopped fresh parsley

Cook and stir onion in oil in large skillet on medium heat 3 minutes.  Add tomatoes, wine and oregano.  Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes or until thickened.

Add shrimp.  Cook 3 minutes, stirring frequently, until shrimp are pink.  Sprinkle with feta cheese; simmer 1 minute.  Stir in parsley.

                       Spring Garden Bacon Frittata

Recipe By     :n/a
Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Main Dish

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  1         tablespoon  vegetable oil
  2               cups  frozen shredded hash brown potatoes
  6                     eggs -- beaten
  1            package  Oscar Mayer Ready-to-Serve Bacon -- (2.1 oz), cut
                        into 1/2" pieces
     1/3           cup  sliced green onions
  1           teaspoon  minced fresh chives -- (optional)
                        Salt -- to taste
                        Freshly-ground black pepper -- to taste
     1/3           cup  Kraft Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Heat oil in medium nonstick skillet on medium-high heat.  Add potatoes; cook 6 to 8 minutes or until potatoes are warm and beginning to brown, stirring occasionally.  Spread potatoes to evenly cover surface of skillet.

Mix eggs, bacon, onions, chives, salt and pepper in small bowl.  Spoon egg mixture evenly over potatoes.  Reduce heat to medium-low; cover.  Cook 8 minutes or until eggs are set.  Remove from heat.

Sprinkle with cheese.  Slip spatula underneath, tip skillet to loosen and gently slide onto serving plate.  Garnish with tomatoes and additional chives.  Cut into wedges.

This recipe yields 6 servings.

Variation:  Prepare as directed, substituting 12 slices Oscar Mayer Center Cut bacon, cooked for Ready-to-Serve Bacon.

Tip:  To easily cut and separate Ready-to-Serve Bacon, separate slices and stack on top of each other.  Cut into 1/2-inch pieces using clean kitchen shears.
